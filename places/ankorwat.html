Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat, located in Cambodia, is the largest religious monument in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built in the 12th century by the Khmer Empire, this magnificent temple complex is dedicated to both Hindu and Buddhist traditions.

With its iconic lotus-shaped towers, detailed carvings, and breathtaking sunrise views, Angkor Wat is a bucket-list destination for history lovers, spiritual seekers, and photographers alike.

Best Time to Visit

November to March, when the weather is cooler and dry.

Things to Do

  • Watch the sunrise over Angkor Wat.
  • Explore nearby temples like Bayon and Ta Prohm.
  • Learn about Khmer history at Angkor Archaeological Park.
  • Take a tuk-tuk ride around Siem Reap.
