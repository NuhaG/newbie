Bora Bora

Bora Bora, located in French Polynesia, is a tropical paradise known for its turquoise lagoon, lush volcanic peaks, and luxurious overwater bungalows. Often called the “Pearl of the Pacific,” Bora Bora is the ultimate honeymoon and luxury travel destination.

Surrounded by coral reefs, the island offers excellent snorkeling, diving, and water activities, making it one of the most beautiful island getaways in the world.

Best Time to Visit

May to October, during the dry season with pleasant weather.

Things to Do

  • Stay in an overwater bungalow.
  • Snorkel with tropical fish and manta rays.
  • Take a lagoon cruise around the island.
  • Hike Mount Otemanu for panoramic views.
