Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon in Arizona, USA, is one of the most iconic natural wonders of the world. Carved by the Colorado River over millions of years, it stretches 446 km long and plunges over 1,800 meters deep.

Its colorful layered rocks reveal Earth’s geological history, while breathtaking viewpoints, hiking trails, and rafting adventures make it a paradise for adventurers and nature lovers.

Best Time to Visit

March to May and September to November, when temperatures are milder.

Things to Do

  • Visit the South Rim viewpoints like Mather Point.
  • Hike the Bright Angel Trail.
  • Take a helicopter tour for aerial views.
  • Raft along the Colorado River.
