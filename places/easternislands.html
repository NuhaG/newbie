Easter Island

Easter Island, or Rapa Nui, is a remote volcanic island in the Pacific Ocean, famous for its mysterious moai statues. These massive stone figures, carved by the Rapa Nui people centuries ago, remain one of the world’s greatest archaeological wonders.

With its rugged landscapes, volcanic craters, and Polynesian culture, Easter Island is both a historical treasure and a paradise for adventurous travelers.

Best Time to Visit

April to June and October to December, with pleasant weather and fewer tourists.

Things to Do

  • Visit the moai statues at Ahu Tongariki.
  • Explore the Rano Raraku quarry.
  • Relax on Anakena Beach.
  • Learn Polynesian traditions at local festivals.
