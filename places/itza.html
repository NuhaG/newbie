Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza in Mexico is one of the most important cities of the ancient Mayan civilization. Its centerpiece, El Castillo pyramid, is a masterpiece of astronomy and architecture, perfectly aligned with the equinoxes.

Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Chichen Itza reflects the brilliance of the Mayan culture through its temples, ball courts, and sacred cenotes.

Best Time to Visit

November to March, when the weather is cooler and more comfortable.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Kukulkan Pyramid (El Castillo).
  • Explore the Great Ball Court.
  • Swim in nearby cenotes.
  • Learn Mayan history with a guided tour.
