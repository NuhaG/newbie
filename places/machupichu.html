Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu in Peru is an ancient Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains. Built in the 15th century, it showcases incredible stonework and terraces with breathtaking mountain views.

Known as the “Lost City of the Incas,” Machu Picchu is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous archaeological sites in the world.

Best Time to Visit

April to October, during the dry season with clear skies.

Things to Do

  • Hike the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu.
  • Climb Huayna Picchu for stunning views.
  • Explore the Temple of the Sun.
  • Learn about Incan culture with a local guide.
