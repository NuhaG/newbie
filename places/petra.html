Petra

Petra, the “Rose City” of Jordan, is a remarkable ancient city carved into pink sandstone cliffs. Once the capital of the Nabatean kingdom, it thrived as a major trading hub.

Today, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, admired for its rock-cut architecture and archaeological significance.

Best Time to Visit

March to May and September to November for pleasant temperatures.

Things to Do

  • Walk through the Siq canyon to the Treasury.
  • Visit the Monastery, Petra’s largest monument.
  • Climb to the High Place of Sacrifice.
  • See Petra by Night, lit with candles.
