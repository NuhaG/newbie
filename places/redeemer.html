Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, is one of the most iconic monuments in the world. Standing 30 meters tall atop Mount Corcovado, the statue symbolizes peace and Christianity.

Overlooking the stunning city of Rio, the statue is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit for anyone exploring Brazil.

Best Time to Visit

December to March (summer in Brazil) for lively atmosphere, though mornings are best to avoid crowds.

Things to Do

  • Take the train through Tijuca Forest up to Corcovado.
  • Capture panoramic views of Rio de Janeiro.
  • Visit nearby Sugarloaf Mountain.
  • Enjoy Brazilian culture and cuisine in Rio.
