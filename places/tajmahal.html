Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal in Agra, India, is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world and a symbol of eternal love. Built in the 17th century by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, the white marble mausoleum is an architectural masterpiece.

Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Taj Mahal is admired for its perfect symmetry, detailed carvings, and stunning gardens.

Best Time to Visit

October to March, when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Things to Do

  • See the Taj at sunrise for magical views.
  • Explore the Mughal gardens and fountains.
  • Visit the Agra Fort nearby.
  • Shop for marble handicrafts in Agra markets.
