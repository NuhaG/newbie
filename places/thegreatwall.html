Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is the longest wall in the world, stretching over 21,000 km. Built across centuries to protect Chinese states from invasions, it remains one of the most incredible engineering feats in history.

Today, it symbolizes China’s strength and cultural heritage, attracting millions of visitors who hike along its ancient paths.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April–May) and Autumn (September–October) for pleasant weather.

Things to Do

  • Hike the Mutianyu or Jinshanling sections.
  • Take a cable car ride for sweeping views.
  • Visit nearby villages for cultural experiences.
  • Capture sunrise or sunset from the wall.
