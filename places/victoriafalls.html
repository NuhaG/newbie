Victoria Falls

Victoria Falls, located on the border of Zimbabwe and Zambia, is one of the largest and most spectacular waterfalls in the world. Known locally as “The Smoke That Thunders,” it is an awe-inspiring sight of nature’s power.

Surrounded by lush rainforests and national parks, Victoria Falls offers both breathtaking scenery and thrilling adventure activities.

Best Time to Visit

February to May, when the falls are at their fullest after the rainy season.

Things to Do

  • Take a helicopter ride over the falls.
  • Go white-water rafting on the Zambezi River.
  • Visit Victoria Falls National Park.
  • Swim at Devil’s Pool (seasonal).
